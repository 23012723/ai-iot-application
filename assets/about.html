About Diabetes Health Tracker

Our Mission

We aim to empower people with Type 2 diabetes by helping them monitor their daily food intake, physical activity, and heart rate. Our goal is to provide users with the tools to make healthier lifestyle choices and manage their diabetes more effectively.

How It Works

The app collects data on your daily activities, including food consumption, movement, and heart rate, to provide insights on calorie intake and calories burned. With personalized recommendations, you can adjust your lifestyle to better manage your diabetes.
