Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my food intake?

You can log your food intake by navigating to the 'Log Food' section and entering the type and weight of the food you've consumed.

Can this app help me manage my diabetes?

Yes! The app provides insights into your daily calorie intake and calories burned, helping you make informed decisions to manage your Type 2 diabetes through lifestyle changes.
